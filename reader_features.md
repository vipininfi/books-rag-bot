# Reader Feature Guide

## Current Features

- **Author Discovery**: Browse a list of all authors and read their professional bios.
- **One-Click Subscription**: Instantly gain access to an author's entire library of books.
- **Subscription Management**: View and manage all active author subscriptions in one place.
- **Semantic Search**: Search for concepts and meanings across all subscribed books simultaneously.
- **AI Reading Assistant**: Ask questions and get answers based exclusively on your subscribed library.
- **Verified Citations**: Every AI answer includes direct links to book titles, chapters, and page numbers.
- **Integrated PDF Viewer**: Read books directly in the browser with a high-performance viewer.
- **Voice Interaction**: Ask questions using voice commands and listen to AI answers via text-to-speech.

## Future Roadmap

- **Instant Summarization**: Generate quick summaries for entire books or specific chapters.
- **Cross-Author Comparisons**: Compare different authors' perspectives on the same topic using AI.
- **Study Guide Generator**: Automatically create quizzes and flashcards from book content.
- **Reading Progress Tracker**: Save your place in every book and track your reading history.
- **AI Recommendations**: Get suggestions for new authors and books based on your interests.
- **Personal Knowledge Base**: Save and organize AI insights and book snippets for future reference.
- **Smart Annotations**: Highlight text and add personal notes that the AI can understand.
- **Research Reports**: Export AI answers and citations into professional PDF or Word documents.
- **Mobile Companion App**: A dedicated mobile experience for reading and AI interaction on the go.
- **Offline Mode**: Access books and cached AI responses without an internet connection.


# Author Feature Guide

## Current Features

- **Secure Book Upload**: Support for PDF files up to 100MB with automated processing.
- **AI Processing Pipeline**: Automated text extraction, semantic chunking, and high-quality embedding generation.
- **Library Management**: A dedicated dashboard to view, manage, and track the processing status of all uploaded books.
- **Vector Search Integration**: Books are automatically indexed in a high-performance vector database for instant reader access.
- **Author Profile & Bio**: Manage a public-facing profile and bio that readers see when browsing the platform.
- **Book Deletion**: Easily remove books from the library, with automatic cleanup of all associated AI data and embeddings.
- **Integrated Preview**: View and read your own books directly in the browser using the built-in PDF viewer.
- **Usage Transparency**: Track how your content is being utilized through the platform's token and cost tracking system.

## Future Roadmap

- **Advanced Analytics**: Detailed insights into which books are most popular and what topics readers are searching for.
- **Reader Engagement Stats**: See how many readers have subscribed to your library and their level of interaction.
- **Revenue Dashboard**: Track earnings and subscription payouts (for monetized platforms).
- **Bulk Upload Tools**: Upload and process multiple books simultaneously for faster library building.
- **Content Optimization**: AI-powered suggestions to improve book searchability and answer accuracy.
- **Interactive Author Page**: Customize your public profile with social links, featured books, and custom branding.
- **Direct Reader Feedback**: Receive anonymous feedback or questions from your subscribers to improve your content.
- **AI Writing Assistant**: Use the platform's AI to help draft summaries, blurbs, or even new content based on your existing library.
- **Collaborative Management**: Invite editors or co-authors to help manage your book collection.
- **Automated Social Sharing**: Automatically notify your followers or social media when a new book is published.

# Admin Feature Guide

## Current Features

- **User Management**: Complete control over user accounts, including role assignments (Reader, Author, Superadmin) and account status.
- **Author Management**: Dedicated view to monitor all authors and deep-dive into their specific book collections.
- **Global Library Oversight**: View and manage every book uploaded to the platform, regardless of the author.
- **System Settings Control**: Centralized management of platform-wide parameters, including API keys and file upload limits.
- **Real-Time Global Stats**: Instant visibility into total user growth, author participation, and library size.
- **Database-Backed Usage Tracking**: Granular tracking of all AI operations, token consumption, and estimated costs stored securely in the database.
- **Premium Admin Interface**: A fixed, professional sidebar layout designed for efficient platform administration.
- **Secure Access Control**: Superadmin-only endpoints protected by robust authentication and role-based checks.

## Future Roadmap

- **Advanced Analytics Dashboard**: Interactive charts and visual trends for user activity, revenue, and system performance.
- **Content Moderation Queue**: A streamlined workflow to review, approve, or reject new book uploads before they go live.
- **Financial Management**: Tools to manage subscriptions, track payouts, and view detailed financial reports.
- **System Health Monitoring**: Real-time alerts and status monitoring for AI services, databases, and vector storage.
- **Comprehensive Audit Logs**: A detailed history of all administrative actions for security and accountability.
- **Automated Backup Management**: Schedule and monitor system backups to ensure data integrity.
- **Granular Admin Permissions**: Ability to create sub-admin roles with restricted access to specific management modules.
- **Bulk Communication Tools**: Send platform-wide announcements or targeted notifications to authors and readers.
- **AI Performance Tuning**: Tools to adjust RAG parameters and model configurations for optimized answer quality.
- **Automated Maintenance Tasks**: Schedule routine system cleanups and vector database optimizations.
